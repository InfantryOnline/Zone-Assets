~4~B Upgrade Success Rates ~B

Each weapon and armor upgrade in Cazzo Forge has a chance succeeding as well as a chance of failing. All upgrade attempts cost the same amount, $10k. Everytime you spend $10k you are essentially rolling the dice to try to get your upgrade.

If you are not a gambling man, there are ways to get a higher % chance of your upgrade succeeding. You may buy "Lucky Dice" off other players or find them while playing Co-Op. Each Lucky Dice you have in your inventory raises your % chance of succeeding by +1. So if you have 10 lucky dice in your inventory, all 10 will be used on your next upgrade and you will have a 10% higher chance of succeeding. If you do not wish to use the Dice, simply drop them before you upgrade.

There also plans for future rare items will permanently help increase success rates.
